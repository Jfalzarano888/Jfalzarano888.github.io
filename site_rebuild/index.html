Hello: My Name is Ziosk!

Ziosk is an interactive tool to help create a smooth and fun dining experience! Read below to examine all the wonderful tasks it can perform!

View

Use Ziosk to view menu items such as appetizers, desserts, and even wine! If you see something you like, feel free to order it directly from Ziosk!

Entertain

Use Ziosk to enjoy news updates and involve your friends in family in a variety of games! Ziosk is a great way to liven up your dinner!

Payment

In a rush and can't find your server? With Ziosk you can pay directly at the table! Just slide your credit card, choose your tip, and print the receipt!
